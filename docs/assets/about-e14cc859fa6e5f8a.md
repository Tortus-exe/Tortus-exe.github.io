By trade, I am a Computer Engineer, so languages close to the metal have a special place in my heart. In particular, the concept of using APL as a machine language for a machine has captivated me. I hope to design a machine with that instruction set one day.

In addition to programming languages, I also enjoy studying human languages. I speak English, Cantonese, and Japanese. I am a native English speaker (as you can hopefully tell!), a heritage speaker of Cantonese, and only an intermediate speaker of Japanese. As my studies in languages went forward, I began to accrue a greater appreciation of language in general. I now hope to use this website to make it easier for people who can speak English to learn Cantonese. Long live the freedom of the Hong Kong people!
